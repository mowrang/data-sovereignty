# Competitors - Quick Reference

## 🏆 Top 5 Competitors

### 1. **Teal** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

- **Focus:** Resume + job search management
- **Strengths:** All-in-one platform, good UX
- **Weakness:** No Google Docs integration
- **Pricing:** $9-29/month

### 2. **Rezi** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

- **Focus:** ATS optimization, keywords
- **Strengths:** Strong ATS matching
- **Weakness:** Resume-only, no job search
- **Pricing:** $7-29/month

### 3. **Jobscan** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

- **Focus:** ATS optimization specialist
- **Strengths:** Best ATS optimization
- **Weakness:** No resume builder, no job search
- **Pricing:** $49.95/month

### 4. **Kickresume** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

- **Focus:** Fast resume generation
- **Strengths:** GPT-4 powered, fast
- **Weakness:** Template-focused, no job search
- **Pricing:** Free + $5-15/month

### 5. **cvtailor.ai** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

- **Focus:** Instant AI tailoring
- **Strengths:** Pay-as-you-go, flexible
- **Weakness:** Limited features
- **Pricing:** Pay-per-use
